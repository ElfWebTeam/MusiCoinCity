"MusiCoinCity" Project Information

  • Project Name: MusiCoinCity
  • Project Track: Eco-friendly
  • Team Name: ElfWeb
  • Dorahacks Project Link: dorahacks.io/buidl/5080
  • Project Goal: A platform that enables users to experience various natural sound environments and to donate to organizations that help preserve these environments.
  • Project Info: This site allows users to experience the sounds of four different natural environments, making it feel like they are in those locations. Users can connect their wallet and make donations to support or improve the environments associated with the sounds they enjoy.
  • Project Website: www.musicoincity.com
  • Project Test Instructions: Log in to the site via the provided link, select an environment from the four options, and if you wish to donate, connect your Metamask wallet and make a donation using the EDU token on the Open Campus Codex Chain.
  • Smart Contract: The platform uses multiple wallets: one main wallet and four separate wallets for each environment. Project Details

To include the Open Campus Codex Chain in your Metamask wallet and make a donation, click the "Wallet" button on the top right of our site and follow the instructions provided there.

Users can experience all four environments and donate to the respective environmental projects of their choice. Donations will be forwarded to the supporting institutions once a sufficient amount is collected.
