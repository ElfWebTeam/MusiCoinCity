How to Install Metamask and Add Open Campus Codex Chain

To install Metamask on your browser and add the Open Campus Codex Chain, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Metamask website (https://metamask.io/) and click on the "Get Chrome Extension" button if you're using Google Chrome or "Get Firefox Extension" if you're using Mozilla Firefox.
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" or "Add to Firefox" and follow the prompts to install Metamask on your browser.
  3. Once installed, click on the Metamask icon in your browser's toolbar and create a new wallet or import an existing one.
  4. Follow these instructions to add the Open Campus Codex Chain to your Metamask wallet:
    • Open Metamask and go to the settings.
    • Select "Networks" and click "Add Network".
    • Fill in the following details:
      • Network Name: Open Campus Codex Chain
      • New RPC URL: https://open-campus-codex-sepolia.drpc.org
      • Chain ID: 0xA045C
      • Currency Symbol: EDU
      • Block Explorer URL: https://opencampus-codex.blockscout.com
  5. Join this website "example.com" and convert your currency to EDU (Open Campus Token) to save it on Metamask.

Once you have completed these steps, you will be able to send and receive EDU on the Open Campus Codex Chain using your Metamask wallet. Remember to always verify the recipient address before sending any transactions and to keep your seed phrase safe and secure.
